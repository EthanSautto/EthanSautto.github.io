Founded in 2024 in the scenic town of Ojai, California, Sauttoworks was born out of a passion for restoring and preserving the iconic cars of the 90s. Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality aftermarket parts and components that help automotive enthusiasts bring their favorite era of cars back to life. Whether you're a weekend warrior restoring a beloved project or a professional looking for precision parts, we’ve got you covered with a range of meticulously crafted products designed to meet the needs of the modern enthusiast.

At Sauttoworks, we understand that the 90s was a pivotal decade in automotive history, with cars that represent the perfect blend of innovation, design, and performance. From Japanese sportscars to American muscle and European classics, our team is dedicated to ensuring that these legendary machines can continue to be driven, enjoyed, and celebrated for years to come. We combine cutting-edge technology with timeless craftsmanship to create parts that not only restore the original spirit of these cars but enhance their performance and durability for today’s drivers.

Our roots are grounded in the heart of California’s car culture, where we draw inspiration from the roads, the people, and the iconic vehicles that have made this era unforgettable. Every part we produce is a testament to our commitment to quality, precision, and the automotive community.
